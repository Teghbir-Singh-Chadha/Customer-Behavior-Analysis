# Customer-Behavior-Analysis
## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing consumer shopping behavior for a retail company to uncover trends, understand customer preferences, and identify factors that influence purchasing decisions. By leveraging data analytics techniques across Python, SQL, and Power BI, the project transforms raw retail transaction data into actionable business insights that can support strategic decision-making.

The analysis explores customer demographics, product categories, discounts, customer reviews, seasonal trends, frequency of purchases, and repeat purchase behavior. The primary objective is to help the company improve customer engagement, optimize marketing campaigns, enhance product offerings, and increase overall profitability.

## Business Problem

A leading retail company has observed changing consumer purchasing patterns and seeks to better understand the drivers behind customer decisions and loyalty. The company wants to determine how various factors such as discounts, product ratings, seasons, and payment preferences impact sales performance and repeat purchases.

### Key Business Question

**"How can the company leverage consumer shopping data to identify trends, improve customer engagement, and optimize marketing and product strategies?"**

## Project Objectives

* Cleaned, transformed, and prepared retail data for analysis using Python.
* Designed and queried a structured database using SQL to extract meaningful business insights.
* Analyzed customer segments, purchasing behavior, and other features.
* Built an interactive Power BI dashboard to visualize key performance indicators and trends.

## Tools & Technologies

* **Python** – Data cleaning, preprocessing, and exploratory data analysis.
* **SQL (MySQL)** – Data modeling, business transaction simulation, and analytical queries.
* **Power BI** – Interactive dashboards and visual storytelling.
* **GitHub** – Version control and project documentation.

## Expected Outcomes

* Identification of high-value customer segments.
* Understanding of factors influencing purchase decisions.
* Recommendations for targeted marketing and product optimization strategies.

This project demonstrates an end-to-end data analytics workflow, from raw data preparation to business intelligence reporting, enabling stakeholders to make informed and data-driven decisions.
